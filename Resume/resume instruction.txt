📌📌  Few Resume Instructions for all 📌📌

1.	Use different formats for different locations.

2.	Use totally different mobile numbers on all resumes.

3.	Use totally different mail ids on all resumes

4.	Shuffle all key skills and experience summery in different resumes.

5.	In work history write properly your designation, company name with located city, and duration (since to till now)

6.	In education if your education is Non- technical then do not mention your branch or stream in resume.  Just add line like completed bachelor’s degree from ________university and _____ college name.  

7.	If you are not comfortable with some skills then you can remove and add which are related to you. 

8.	Provided two project but its not mandatory to use two project for everyone. 

9.	Do not mention more than 2 projects in your resume.


10.	If two projects are there then there is no need of separate roles and responsibilities of projects. You can simply add two project name and domain and below that you can add responsibilities. 


11.	Remove client name and description of all project (only project name and responsibilities should be there)

12.	Don’t add extra theory in resume anywhere. 

13.	No need of personal information in every resume.

14.	Don’t add your permanent, current address etc. things in resume. Just mention profile city name in front of company name In work history.

NOTE: Minimum 3 Resumes are mandatory for high call frequency.
